Closing thoughts

Thank you to everyone who used Emerald! I honestly just made this for my AP project and didn't expect anyone to actually use it but it turned out to be a kinda fun yet shortlived platform. It has become too expensive for me to run it anymore but I'm happy with what came from it and I hope to do more things like this in the future! I still have all the data backed up so there's a chance I'll bring it back if I make the tech a little more cost efficient, though it's unlikely.


Also, since some people asked, all passwords were totally safe (hashed & salted) and no one including myself was ever able to view them. I made it a goal to make sure things were very secure and if there's anything that works on this thing it's definitely the security.


That aside, I ran this at such a loss so yeah I'm totally selling your data give me $5 and I'll send you all database apart from hashed passwords and IP addresses. For inquiries email me at contact@gavhern.com where I will probably laugh at you for taking this seriously and then promptly take your money.


Thanks everyone for making this thing kinda fun lol 💖. Emerald lives on in our hearts and also on my hard drive.
